Message from the President

Tradition since 2003

First of all, I am very honored to welcome you to James Cook University (JCU).

As a world-renowned institution of higher learning, James Cook University has a high degree of integration with related industries in teaching and cutting-edge scientific research results, so it is world-renowned. I firmly believe that the students will embark on a new, exciting and successful overseas study journey.

As a direct campus of James Cook University, the Singapore campus has achieved outstanding results since its establishment in 2003. In 2015, it obtained the EduTrust Star certification issued by the Singapore government and became the first institution in Singapore to obtain the highest level of certification. The JCU Singapore campus has achieved outstanding results in all key parts of the assessment. The evaluation process includes a comprehensive examination of the academic level of the Singapore campus, the students' school experience, and the daily operation and management of the university. In 2019, the JCU Singapore campus successfully renewed the EduTrust Star certification, which also proves that we have continued to do so in the four years since we obtained the certification in 2015. Setting the highest standards and strictest requirements, it has always been in the benchmark position of higher education in Singapore and is highly recognized by the governments of China, New Zealand and Australia. We are committed to maintaining this status, which is also a commitment to our students to meet everyone's high expectations for academics and various aspects of management.

Introduction to Singapore campus


Business management

The MBA program consists of 12 subjects, covering various fields of modern management. Students can choose one of the six majors for specialization, or choose to take a comprehensive course. The course aims to teach innovative business thinking models, equip students with data-driven decision-making skills, and engage students in experiential learning to meet the needs of the business environment.


Analysis and business solutions

With the rise of big data, companies increasingly need managers who can quickly analyze and understand massive amounts of information. The project provides students with advanced tools and techniques for data classification and analysis, and enables them to make a beneficial impact on the business based on the data. Graduates can engage in analysis and business decision-making work, such as business analysis, consulting, finance, supply chain management, operation management, marketing and other business fields.


Innovative marketing

The project is centered on data-driven creativity, combining marketing knowledge with advanced management and analysis skills. Students will master a data-driven approach to understand the market, competitors, and product mix. Students can learn cutting-edge operating techniques and skills through practical projects and activities. Graduates can serve as brand managers, marketing managers, product managers, strategic marketing managers, digital marketing managers, and e-commerce managers.


Entrepreneurship

This project is designed for students who intend to start a business. Students will be exposed to cutting-edge trends, tools, technologies, theories and practices to identify, improve, incubate and develop new business ideas. Graduates can start new businesses, hold leadership positions in start-up companies, or work in venture capital companies.


Global talent management

Countries are investing in innovative and sustainable global talent management to gain an advantage in the fierce competition for talent. The project equips students with professional knowledge and practical tools of human resources, ethical standards of human resources and social responsibility, global vision, and the ability of team building and problem solving. This project is not only suitable for employers, managers and professionals who study/work in the human resources department, but also suitable for people who are interested in human resources and interpersonal communication.

Exhibition, tourism and hotel management

The project provides students with an international perspective on the tourism industry, and provides analytical tools and creative solutions for managers in the tourism, hotel, and exhibition industries. Graduates will acquire the corresponding skills and knowledge to work efficiently or transfer to the hotel and tourism industry.

International Tourism and Hotel Management and Business Administration Joint

Graduates can meet the needs of the world's top hotel management organizations for senior management positions.
